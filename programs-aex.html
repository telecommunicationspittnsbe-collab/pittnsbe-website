AEX — Academic Excellence

Supporting members' academic success through study sessions, tutoring, and accountability.

Overview

What this program does

Our Academic Excellence (AEX) Chair is responsible for maintaining the academic standards within our chapter and providing opportunities for students to improve academically. They do this through weekly 'Study Jam' activities, often held in the Hillman Library, in which students can collaborate with each other to improve their understanding of any confusing topics. Additional, the AEX Chair conducts our bi-annual Study-A-Thon activities, where professors hold study sessions during the last week of the semester to help students better prepare for their finals.
