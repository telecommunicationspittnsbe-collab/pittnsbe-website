Pre-College Initiative

PCI

Our two Pre-College Initiative (PCI) chairs aim to encourage young K-12 students to pursue and excel in engineering-related futures. They do this by creating opportunities to engage with local NSBE Jr. chapters in the Pittsburgh area through STEM activities and teachings, as well as hosting events within our chapter to encourage outreach.
